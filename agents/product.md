# Scoutly Product Agent

## Vision
Scoutly is a youth sports recruiting and social platform starting with volleyball. It helps athletes showcase their skills, manage college recruiting pipelines, and build their social presence.

## Core User
Jake Wolf — Libero, Class of 2029, Rockstar Volleyball 16U. A high school athlete with elite stats looking to get recruited by top programs.

## Tabs
1. **Profile** — Athlete identity card, stats, highlight reel, coach endorsements
2. **Recruiting** — School pipeline tracker, email generator, follow-up tips
3. **Feed** — Social posts, news cards, post composer with broadcast toggles

## Success Metrics
- All interactions work (toggles, counters, threads, modals)
- Mobile-first, 420px max-width
- Fast and snappy with no layout shift
